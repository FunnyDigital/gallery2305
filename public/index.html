Painter and Sculptor

Larry Graeber is a Texas-based artist, born in San Antonio.

Graeber began exhibiting in 1971 curated into the Texas Painting and Sculpture 71 Exhibition, Dallas Museum of Fine Arts. His first major one-person exhibition was in 1974, Works From a Small Duplex, McNay Art Museum curated by director John Leeper. Besides other publications, his work is Included in the book Art At Our Doorstep and Texas Abstract, Modern/ Contemporary, and continues to exhibit in group and one-person shows regularly. Graeber also paints in west Texas where he has a small studio.
