Gallery2305 Lagos has been specializing in African art and Old Master paintings for over 35 years. Our gallery, located in Nigeria's historic Lagos city is one of the largest Private commercial gallery spaces in Lekki and showcases 500 years of African art and Sculptures from the Different periods through to the late 20th century.

Spread across three floors, our gallery is a testament to the development of fine art in Africa over the past 500 years. The gallery is known for diligent research and connoisseurship and regularly stages major exhibitions accompanied by scholarly publications.
