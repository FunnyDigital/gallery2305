WAR OF THE ROSES

This love story is an organic feel of building up layers and textures with a color palette that naturally combines the direction of my thoughts with the freedom to harmonize colors of semi-abstract acts in deep conversations of positions for characters mostly in my sub consciousness that leaves you with a story of rebirth spanning from another life of recycled thoughts.

The theme of these pieces captures sweet living nectar, rose petals, ray of blossom and temperament of scents with enchanted unbiased characteristics invoked by a sense of deep admiration for colors and femininity.

My War of the Roses series is sensitive yet assertive, beautiful yet broken, wild yet tamed, refined yet imperfect, playful yet with thorns, feminine yet uncultured and happy yet sadly interpreted.
