Art of Street hawking

The streets would feel uninhabited and dull without the daily interactions of people. Street hawking changes the perspective and builds a feel and a need.
The environment is the canvas and the vendor is the artist. In a way the vendor creates a feel that is unique and particular the anyone who encounter his work, it could be a lasting memory that would keep you coming back or just the way the presence gives life to the environment.
